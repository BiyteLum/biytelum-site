Analysis Coverage

Our review covers all key GDPR and LGPD disclosure requirements:
  • Data controller identity and contact details - Is it clear who controls the data?
  • Purposes of data processing - Are processing purposes clearly stated?
  • Legal basis for processing - Is the lawful basis identified for each purpose?
  • Data categories and sources - Are data types and collection methods clear?
  • Data retention periods - Are retention timeframes specified?
  • Individual rights information - Are data subject rights properly explained?
  • Data sharing and transfers - Are third-party disclosures transparent?
  • Contact information - Can individuals easily reach you with questions?
  • Brazilian market considerations (LGPD) - ANPD requirements and local specifications
