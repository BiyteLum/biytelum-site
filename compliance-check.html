Assessment Coverage

We evaluate your privacy practices across eight key areas:
Privacy Policies
Do you have clear, compliant privacy notices that properly inform individuals about data processing?
Consent Mechanisms
Are your consent collection methods valid, specific, and properly documented?
Data Mapping
Do you know what personal data you collect, process, and share?
Individual Rights
Can you handle data subject requests for access, deletion, and portability?
Data Security
Are appropriate technical and organizational measures in place to protect data?
Vendor Management
Are data processing agreements in place with third-party vendors?
Breach Response
Do you have procedures to detect, report, and respond to data breaches?
Record Keeping
Are processing activities documented as required by GDPR Article 30?
