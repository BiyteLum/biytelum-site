EU AI ACT COMPLIANCE PROGRAM

Regulatory Readiness for European Markets

Navigate the EU AI Act's complex requirements with expert guidance and practical implementation strategies. The EU AI Act introduces comprehensive regulations for AI systems based on risk categories, with specific obligations for high-risk AI applications.

Compliance Framework Includes: AI system classification, conformity assessment procedures, quality management systems, risk mitigation protocols, transparency requirements, and post-market surveillance.

High-Risk AI Systems Coverage: Biometric identification, critical infrastructure management, educational assessment, employment decisions, essential services access, law enforcement tools, and safety component management.

Implementation Timeline: 6-12 months for comprehensive compliance program including documentation, testing protocols, and ongoing monitoring systems.

Essential for organizations deploying AI systems in European markets or serving European customers.
