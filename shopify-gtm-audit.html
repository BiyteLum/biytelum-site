THE PROBLEM

Why Most E-commerce Tracking Fails Privacy Compliance

Your Shopify store is probably tracking customers before they consent. Your Google Tag Manager is likely firing duplicate events. Your attribution is broken, and you don't know which marketing channels are actually working.

"We understand both privacy compliance requirements and how GTM tracking works. We can spot the issues most people miss."

Common issues we find:

  • Tags firing before consent is captured (GDPR violation)
  • Duplicate conversion events inflating reported revenue
  • Broken data layer implementations causing attribution gaps
  • Third-party scripts loading without proper consent controls
  • Customer data sent to analytics before consent banner loads
  • Consent mode misconfigured or not implemented at all
  • Server-side tracking bypassing consent entirely

These aren't theoretical risks. They're compliance violations that expose you to regulatory action and undermine your ability to trust your own data.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Common Questions
Do you fix the issues you find?

No. This is an audit and recommendations service only. We identify issues and tell you what needs fixing, but we don't make changes to your GTM or Shopify setup. You'll need to implement the recommendations yourself or hire a technical partner.

What access do you need?

We work from GTM container exports (JSON file) and screenshots/documentation of your Shopify tracking setup. We don't need direct access to your accounts. You provide the exports and documentation to us.

How technical is the audit?

We identify consent compliance issues, duplicate events, and tracking configuration problems. The report is written for business owners and marketers, not just developers. We explain what's wrong and what needs fixing in plain language.

Will this audit tell me if I'm GDPR compliant?

We review your tracking setup against GDPR consent requirements and flag issues. This is educational guidance, not a legal compliance determination. For legal opinions on GDPR compliance, consult qualified legal counsel.

What if I need help implementing the recommendations?

We provide clear recommendations but don't do the implementation work. You'll need to either implement the changes yourself or hire a GTM/Shopify developer. We can suggest where to find qualified help if needed.

Is this service right for me if I'm not technical?

Yes. The audit identifies problems and explains what needs fixing. You can take our recommendations to your developer, freelancer, or agency to handle implementation. The value is knowing what's broken and what to fix.
