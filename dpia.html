What is a DPIA?

A Data Protection Impact Assessment (DPIA) is a process designed to identify and minimize privacy risks in data processing activities. Under GDPR Article 35, DPIAs are mandatory for processing operations that are likely to result in high risks to individuals' rights and freedoms.

Our DPIA Sprint service provides a streamlined, professional approach to conducting these assessments, ensuring your organization meets regulatory requirements while implementing practical privacy safeguards.

⚠️

When DPIAs are Required

GDPR Article 35 requires DPIAs for processing activities that are likely to result in high risk to individuals. You need a DPIA when:

  • Systematic monitoring of publicly accessible areas on a large scale
  • Large-scale processing of special categories of data or criminal convictions
  • Systematic evaluation or scoring including profiling with legal or significant effects
  • Automated decision-making with legal or similarly significant effects
  • Processing biometric or genetic data for identification purposes
  • Combining datasets from different sources
  • Processing vulnerable individuals' data (children, employees, patients)
  • Using new technologies or innovative applications

When in doubt, conducting a DPIA demonstrates due diligence and proactive privacy compliance.
