ASSESSMENT METHODOLOGY

Systematic Privacy Review Process

Our privacy gap assessment follows a proven methodology developed through hundreds of enterprise privacy evaluations across multiple industries and regulatory environments.

PHASE 01
DISCOVERY & SCOPING

Initial stakeholder interviews, regulatory scope determination, and assessment planning. Document review and data inventory preparation.

PHASE 02
POLICY & PROCESS REVIEW

Comprehensive review of privacy policies, procedures, vendor agreements, and governance frameworks against regulatory requirements.

PHASE 03
TECHNICAL CONTROLS EVALUATION

Assessment of data security measures, access controls, encryption implementation, and technical privacy safeguards.

PHASE 04
DATA FLOW MAPPING

Comprehensive mapping of personal data collection, processing, storage, and sharing activities across systems and third parties.

PHASE 05
GAP ANALYSIS & SCORING

Detailed compliance gap identification with risk scoring, regulatory citation, and impact assessment for each finding.

PHASE 06
STRATEGIC ROADMAP

Prioritized remediation plan with timelines, resource requirements, and implementation guidance for addressing identified gaps.
