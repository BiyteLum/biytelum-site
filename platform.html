What it is

Operational compliance for agentic AI

Autonomous AI agents make decisions, call tools, access data, and act across jurisdictions — often without a human in the loop. BiyteLüm is the monitoring layer that keeps those agents accountable. Connect your agents via API, and every action is evaluated against our multi-framework policy engine in real time.

📡
REAL-TIME LOG INGESTION
Agents send structured audit logs via POST /api/ingest. Each action — tool calls, data access, decisions, outcomes — is captured and evaluated instantly.
⚖️
23-RULE POLICY ENGINE
Every log is evaluated against 23 active policy rules spanning MAESTRO (7 layers), EU AI Act, NIST AI RMF, ISO 42001, and LATAM regulations. Violations are caught the moment they occur.
🔢
INTEGRITY SCORING (0–100)
Each agent receives a live integrity score. Tier 1 violations cap the score at 49 until resolved. 30-day trend history shows whether agent behavior is improving or degrading over time.
🗂️
MAESTRO 7-LAYER ASSESSMENT
The Cloud Security Alliance MAESTRO framework maps threats across Model, Training Data, Architecture, System Integration, Operations, Human Oversight, and Incident Response layers.
🌍
MULTI-JURISDICTION COVERAGE
Policies enforce across 15+ jurisdictions: EU/EEA (GDPR + EU AI Act), Brazil (LGPD + AI Bill 2338), Chile, Colombia, Mexico, Argentina, and NIST-aligned global rules.
📄
REGULATORY EVIDENCE & PDF REPORTS
Generate downloadable PDF compliance reports — executive summary, violation tables, MAESTRO coverage, and EU AI Act readiness checklist — ready for regulators, auditors, and insurers.
🔗
CHAIN VIOLATION TRACKING
Multi-agent call sequences are traced end-to-end. Violations are linked across parent and child agents so you can see exactly where in an agentic pipeline a failure originated.
🛠️
REMEDIATION PLAYBOOKS
Every violation includes a step-by-step remediation playbook. Your engineering and compliance teams know exactly what to fix — not just that something is broken.
👥
TEAM & ROLE MANAGEMENT
Admin, Compliance Officer, Engineer, and Viewer roles. Invite teammates, manage access, and maintain an immutable audit trail of every compliance action your team takes.

How It Works

Connect in minutes. Monitor continuously.

BiyteLüm integrates via a single API endpoint. No agents to install, no SDKs to learn.

GENERATE AN API KEY
Create a blm_... key in the platform dashboard under Connect. One key per environment or agent pipeline.
SEND AUDIT LOGS
Your agent sends structured JSON logs to POST /api/ingest — agent ID, action type, permissions, jurisdiction, reasoning trace, outcome.
POLICY ENGINE EVALUATES
Each log is instantly evaluated against 23 rules. Violations are detected, categorized by tier, and mapped to the relevant MAESTRO layer and regulatory article.
INTEGRITY SCORE UPDATES
The agent's integrity score is recomputed. Tier 1 violations cap at 49/100. Trend history updates. Alerts fire via email, Slack, or custom webhook.
RESOLVE & REPORT
Compliance officers review violations with remediation playbooks, resolve them with one click, and download PDF reports for regulators or internal audits.
