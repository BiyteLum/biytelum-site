About BiyteLüm

Sensitive Data. High Stakes.
Real Experience.

BiyteLüm was founded by a former US federal adjudications officer who spent over six years processing asylum applications, humanitarian parole cases, and refugee petitions at USCIS. We understand sensitive human data — not from a textbook, but from years of collecting, assessing, and protecting it under strict federal regulatory frameworks.

That same precision now serves organizations navigating privacy compliance and legal professionals seeking expert advisory support in humanitarian immigration cases. Two practice areas. One through-line: protecting people's data when the stakes are real.

The name BiyteLüm combines byte — the fundamental unit of information — with lüm, from loom: the weaving of threads into something whole.
