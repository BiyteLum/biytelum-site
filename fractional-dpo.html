What is a Fractional DPO?

A Fractional Data Protection Officer (DPO) provides professional privacy leadership on a part-time, contract basis. Instead of hiring a full-time privacy officer, organizations can access experienced CIPP/E, CIPM & CIPT certified professionals for strategic guidance and compliance oversight.

Our fractional DPO service is perfect for startups and growing companies that need ongoing privacy expertise but don't have the budget or workload to justify a full-time privacy hire.

💡

Fractional vs Full-Time DPO

Fractional DPO Benefits:

  • Cost-effective: Pay only for hours you need
  • Immediate expertise: No hiring or training time
  • Flexible commitment: Scale up or down as needed
  • Fresh perspective: External viewpoint on privacy challenges
  • Diverse experience: Cross-industry knowledge

Full-Time DPO Challenges:

  • High cost: $120K+ annual salaries plus benefits
  • Limited talent pool: Hard to find qualified candidates
  • Underutilized: May not have enough work for 40 hours/week
  • Single perspective: One person's experience and approach
  • Training required: Company-specific systems and processes
